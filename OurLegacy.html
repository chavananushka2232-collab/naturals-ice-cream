Our Story

"If I want to sell ice cream, I should invest in trees."

Our Founder

Mr. Raghunandan Kamath

Growing up in Mangalore, surrounded by mango orchards and fresh fruits, he discovered something simple yet revolutionary — ice cream should contain real fruit.

1984

Our History

The journey started with a small parlour in Juhu, Mumbai. Every scoop was handcrafted using real fruits, milk and sugar.

The Idea

Real Fruit. Real Taste.

One question changed everything: "Why should fruit ice cream only have fruit flavour? Why not real fruit?"

1994

Growing Across India

What started with 12 flavours became one of India's most loved ice cream brands.
